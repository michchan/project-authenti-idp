Sign out of TaskMaster Demo only
Keep me signed in to other AuthentiIDP applications. You'll stay logged in to Analytics, Shopping List, and other connected apps.

What Happens Next

After signing out of TaskMaster only:

  • Your session in this app will end
  • Protected content will be inaccessible
  • You'll be redirected to the landing page
  • Other AuthentiIDP apps will remain accessible

After signing out of all applications:

  • All active sessions will be terminated
  • You'll be signed out of every connected app
  • Fresh login required for any app access
  • Maximum security and privacy protection

To sign back in: Use the same credentials or SSO from another connected app
